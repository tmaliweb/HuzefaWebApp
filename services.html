Our Services at
Kapadia Dental Care

General dentistry

Kapadia dental care provides treatment options for every patient’s circumstances. If you have a family in Detroit, you can enjoy comprehensive care that is aimed toward protecting perfect smiles.

Dental Exam and Consultation

A comprehensive dental examination and consultation at Kapadia Dental care will give you the opportunity to meet the team and discuss your oral health concerns. The team will clean your teeth to reveal a sparkling smile and look for any concerning defects. Once the examination and cleaning concludes, Dr Kapadia will go through any necessary treatment options with you and give you the best solutions for your overall oral health.

Cosmetic dentistry

Everyone wants a beautiful smile and some of us need a little help getting there. A simple teeth whitening procedure can make a huge difference. When your teeth are dull and drab-looking, you appear older than your actual age. Whitening those stained teeth can take years off your appearance. We also provide other cosmetic dentistry services.

Porcelain veneers can mask a variety of minor dental flaws, including teeth that do not respond to whitening. Veneers can make your teeth look brighter and whiter. They can also hide minor misalignments or small gaps between teeth. If you have misshapen teeth or your teeth appear to be too small, porcelain veneers can hide the problem and give you a perfectly straight and beautiful smile. Many of those Hollywood smiles are thanks to porcelain veneers. Ask Dr Kapadia how you can achieve this glamorous look.

Restorative dentistry

When teeth are missing, a variety of dental problems can develop. These problems can affect your oral health and your appearance, not to mention they can make you too self-conscious to smile. Restorative dentistry can correct those problems when teeth are replaced.

Dr Kapadia will take his time and assess the needs for your teeth and will give you the best option for tooth replacement including full or partial dentures, dental bridges and dental implants. Each option has its advantages. Traditional dentures, full and partial, are the most economical method of replacing missing teeth. Fixed bridges are permanently placed and non-removable and they provide a natural look and feel when teeth need to be replaced. The dental implant is also a permanent structure, providing an even more aesthetically-pleasing look and feel. Ask Dr K about the alternatives for replacing your missing teeth. We will help you determine the best options for your situation.

Preventive Dentistry

Preventive dentistry is the branch of dentistry that deals with keeping teeth healthy. At kapadia dental care we have the expertise and training to keep your teeth healthy and give you advice on how to take care of them at home.

We realize it can be hard to convince a lot of people to go to the dentist or regularly take care of their teeth, but it’s best for everyone involved. According to our dentist at Kapadia Dental Care, preventing diseases is a lot easier than treating them. It’s also less costly and takes less time.

Preventive dentistry is very important for children. Children between the ages of 5 and 15 are most liable to getting dental cavities. This is because they don’t take care of their teeth or what they eat. Your child will need dental care from the moment they get their first tooth. Don’t postpone taking him or her to the dentist until they’re teenagers or older.

Oral surgery

If you know that you’re a candidate for oral surgery services, but you’ve been delaying your appointment because of anxiety over the procedure, The dentist at Kapadia Dental Care can counsel you ahead of your treatment regarding sedation options and which type of sedation dentistry might be best suited to help in your anxiety. There are multiple options that an oral surgeon may recommend based on your patient profile. But the main takeaway is that you do not postpone your important dental surgery any longer. Instead, make an appointment with the caring team at Kapadia Dental Care to regain control of your oral health.

Pediatric dentistry

We love kids at Kapadia Dental Care. Your children will enjoy their visit at our office. We love working with children. Our warm and friendly dental team will have your kids relaxed and enjoy their visit to the dentist. Dr Kapadia is very gentle and patient with kids to help make them feel comfortable, your kids will stay happy and calm while our team takes care of their dental needs.

The preventative care your child will receive at Kapadia Dental Care will help those teeth stay strong and healthy. Sealants stop dental decay from developing in the grooves and fissures of children’s teeth. Fluoride treatments help teeth stay strong and fight against cavities. Education on how to brush and floss properly will help in still good oral hygiene habits in your young ones. Ask Dr Kapadia for more details.
