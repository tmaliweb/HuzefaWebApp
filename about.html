About Us

DR. HUZEFA KAPADIA,
DDS
Dentist

WE GIVE YOU MORE OF WHAT YOU WANT, THE TYPE OF DENTAL CARE YOU DESERVE!

Dr. Kapadia offers comprehensive general, cosmetic and restorative dental services for patients of ALL ages. New patients are welcome, including those who have been away from the dentist due to fear and dental anxiety.

Dr Kapadia has dedicated many hours to continuing education in order to provide quality dentistry that can address everything from routine preventive care to damaged and missing teeth. We perform a thorough evaluation of your dental health, including the teeth, gums and jaw joints. This holistic approach is designed to identify any underlying imbalances in the bite that are the cause of common dental problems and may compromise the results of any dentistry you receive.
